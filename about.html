About our organization

What does Swivro do? We create disruptive digital and physical solutions to benefit your everyday life. Every day we wake up, we, at some point during the day, work hard to significantly improve Swivro and its subdivisions such as FinanceDeck, Phobos Space, Anonymize, and soon more.

Discover the Swivro Doctrine

There's no bullsh*t going on here.

The Swivro way. Any products, solutions, or services, whether it be digital or physical, operated by Swivro, are operated in the most fair way possible.

Zero Censorship

Even if we disagree with your opinion, we will never try to censor you on any of our platforms.

Privacy-oriented

Unlike Amazon, Swivro does not and will never violate your digital privacy, no matter what.

Decentralization

We believe in decentralization. To understand, just look at Bitcoin - a digital unseizable currency.

Not focused on profit

Swivro is not focused on profit at all, but, we of course need to make some money to continue Swivro.

The Journey of Swivro

February, 2016

In early 2016, Swivro was founded, where it was originally called DiskNet. The DiskNet group developed software, like web browsers, for Microsoft Windows users.

September, 2019

In September of 2019, we re-branded to Swivro, and the domain swivro.com was registered for the first time, though, today, we're using the domain name swivro.net.

Early-to-mid 2021

In early-to-mid 2021, Swivro slightly changes its purpose and starts leaning more towards the development of aerospace technologies rather than online software and digital privacy.

February, 2022

Swivro receives a major upgrade - getting a brand new logo as well as a brand new modern website, improving your experience while visiting the Swivro website.

The story of Swivro doesn't end here

Swivro will continue to exist for the foreseeable future. We have no plans to shut down, so, unless we become incapacitated, you should expect Swivro to continue to exist. As time goes on, more sections will continue to be added to the timeline above. Interested in contributing to Swivro?
