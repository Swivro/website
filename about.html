About Us

All about your favorite website, Swivro.

What exactly is Swivro? Tell me!

Swivro is an internet-based technology group that was founded in 2016, formerly known as DiskNet. We are privacy oriented. We care about privacy and security. We do our best to ensure your data is secure, safe, and private. We don't unethically share data with any 3rd parties and we cannot be acquired.

We offer privacy-oriented software, products, services, and applications online, and we also post news/info about Swivro progress, and general tech news including website development, software development, and much more.

Why is your logo an elephant?: Elephant's are cool animals.

We consider ourselves a nonprofit group and a "silent organization", pick which ever one you think sounds better in a sentence while mentioning Swivro. When we say nonprofit, we don't refer to being a legal registered nonprofit (IE: 501c3) organization. This just means, making a profit is not our primary goal, and we only choose to make somewhat of a profit to allow ourselves to afford the cost of providing the online free services we provide, as well as to cover the cost of "working time" aka human labor time. We do like doing what we do, but it cost us time & money, and as you know, time is valuable and we have limited time per day. When we call ourselves a "Silent Organization", we mean our team members remain anonymous, including the Owner & Founder of Swivro, we are not a registered nonprofit organization, and we do not actively seek or accept new team members.

Swivro runs many brands, products, and services which currently primarily offer open source and closed source computer and online software like, passwords managers, website builders, code & DNS Hosting platforms, and much more, all to help with your everyday life. You can contact Swivro at any time via support@swivro.com if you have any suggestions, but please do note that we will not reply for security reasons.
