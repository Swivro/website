About Swivro

Ever been curious about what Swivro is? Maybe you found us on Google. Regardless, here's what we do as seen below.

Learn what Swivro is and what Swivro does

Swivro is an online organization focused on cybersecurity, free-speech, online privacy, and anti mass-surveillance. Our mission is to ensure that the internet operates in a way where people are not spied on, are not unethically tracked, can speek freely, in other words; can express their thoughts or feelings without censorship. We help you stay safe from cybercrime and unethical tracking. We show you how companies violate your online privacy, which companies violate your online privacy, alternatives to the companies that violate your online privacy, and much more. Additionally, we provide privacy-focused & free-speech web services such as web hosting, VPN hosting, and more, to protect your online privacy, and to keep you anonymous when browsing the internet or using internet-based services. Swivro is owned by Albert Arnaud as of August 18th, 2021.
