How do you stay anonymous?

To remain as anonymous as possible on the internet, and to keep your online privacy from being compromised, you should follow the three steps seen below.

1

Download a VPN

A bad guy can use your IP Address to see the general area you live in, your internet service provider, and more. VPN Software lets you hide your IP Address. Not looking to pay for a VPN? Try ProtonVPN (has the fastest speeds for a free VPN) or DewVPN.
2

Download Firefox and Tor

Firefox is an open source web browser developed by Mozilla. Firefox doesn't track you. Tor Browser is focused on anonymity, so when accessing sites that you absolutely cannot have anyone know about you visiting; Tor is the best option as it includes multiple proxy layers and lots of encryption.
3

Change your DNS Servers

You're probably using your internet service providers DNS Servers or some other unsecure and unencrypted one. Switch to LibreDNS for increased privacy, they keep no logs and don't monitor what you're doing. What is DNS?
4

You're all done!

Congratulations, in just three steps as seen above, you've majorly improved your online privacy & security. If you really want to stay as anonymous and as private as possible, we recommend checking out https://privacy.promo, a website we own.

Swivro FAQ

How long has Swivro been around?

Swivro was founded in the year 2016 under the name DiskNet. We've operated under a few other names, but we're now known as Swivro and have no plans to change our name any time soon.

Why should I trust you?

We take many precautions to ensure maximum security, privacy, and anonymity for our visitors. When possible, we store as little data as possible from you. You're welcome to access Swivro.Net via Tor without getting blocked.

Is Swivro Free?

"Swivro" itself is not a product, but it offers services, like Speaklink. Most of our products are free whilst some are considered a premium service, requiring a payment in order to use the service.

What do you develop/offer?

Swivro offers online privacy-focused products & services, with no unethical tracking involved whatsoever. Additionally, we also offer open source software available on via our Git organization.
