Safe Crypto Exchanges

Cryptocurrency is the most private way to send payments online. Discover privacy-respecting crypto exchanges today.

Purchase cryptocurrency anonymously, privately, securely, and seamlessly.

As you probably know by now, cryptocurrency the most secure, private, and anonymous way to send payments on the internet. Cryptocurrency also make you money, which we're sure you don't mind. Below, we'll suggest the most reliable & privacy-friendly crypto exchanges.

1. Bisq Network

Buy and sell Bitcoin for fiat (or other cryptocurrencies) privately and securely using Bisq's peer-to-peer network and open-source desktop software. No registration is required, and there is no KYC (know your customer) policy, meaning you do not have to provide any personal/sensitive information about yourself. Bisq never holds your funds. Deposits held in 2-of-2 multisig wallets encourage safe, successful trades. Your data is stored locally on-disk, and is never sent to a central server. Every Bisq node is a Tor hidden service by default. Lastly, Bisq is also open source, with the source code being available on GitHub.

2. LocalCryptos

Hackers have stolen billions of dollars from centralized exchanges. LocalCryptos offers a different approach. Rather than having users trade with LocalCryptos, users trade with each other, which is also what Bisq does (users trade with eachother, not a central company). LocalCryptos lets you buy and sell cryptos using any payment method. Including bank transfer, PayPal, and more. LocalCryptos also provides an encrypted messenger - messages between users are end-to-end encrypted, similar to Signal or Session (two very secure messengers). Unlike Bisq, LocalCryptos is not a desktop app that you must download, it is an online 'app'. It supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and more.

3. Swapzone

Swapzone aggregates crypto-to-crypto exchange offers for supported cryptocurrency pairs through APIs of more than 15 exchange services. Swapzone sends users’ inquiries in; gets the deals directly from their partners, sorting them out by rate, speed and rating; and lets users make swaps via chosen providers without ever leaving the Swapzone website. Unlike LocalCryptos and Bisq, Swapzone supports hundreds of cryptocurrencies. We use Swapzone very often to swap our Bitcoin for Monero, and have never had any significant issues.

4. LocalMonero

Like all of the other exchanges suggested above, on LocalMonero you are dealing with humans. Unlike centralized cryptocurrency exchanges, you make a trade directly with another person. This makes the process lean and fast, as there is no corporate overhead. You get your Monero instantly. Additionally, LocalMonero can support every payment method its user community supports, making it possible for users without access to traditional banking to also be able to buy or sell Monero. For every transaction, LocalMonero offers escrow or transaction service to protect the buyer of Monero.
