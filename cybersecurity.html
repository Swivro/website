Lack of Security

A lot of online companies, websites, apps, etc have poor security, leading to harmful events such as data breaches.

What is the issue?

An incredible amount of online services, websites, and more, all have very poor security. This can lead to server intrusion, data breaches, and more. There's not much you can do about this, but Swivro is helping by continuously reporting security issues to random websites. In the past 3 weeks (as of 10/25/21), we've reported a total of over 135 security flaws to 15 individual websites. Most security issues we've reported are now resolved.

Why even care?

When a website or online service like Facebook (just an example, no need to sue us Zuckerberg!) has poor security, it will likely experience a data breach. If you use that service that experienced a data breach, your personal info stored with that company is now exposed to the public, as a result of the data breach. This can lead to identity theft, fraud, and a lot of other bad things.

How to solve it

You unfortunately can't do much to improve the security of websites, but you should store as little personal info as possible with companies, even if you trust them, so that in the event of a data breach, less of your personal info will be exposed. If you've discovered a security issue with a website or online service, you should contact them immediately.
