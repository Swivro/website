FAQ

Do you operate other brands/services other than the ones listed on this website?

Yes, Swivro does actively maintain other services (other than the ones listed on our services/brands page), but they are not our main focus, hence we don't publicly associate ourselves with some.

Are all your products free?

No. Most of our products are free, but some may be considered premium services & require a payment in order to begin using them.

How can I contribute?

If you have an idea or want to contribute to Swivro, you can either submit your idea to us via support@swivro.com or you can send us a donation :)

How do you handle security threats?

We have no security policy, meaning we are not obligated to inform you about a discovered security issue within a certain amount of time, however, we do our best to inform you under 72 hours.
