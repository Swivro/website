 Secure Email

Swivro Secure Email allows you send, receive, store, and manage emails, like a normal email provider lets you, except with complete user privacy. We don't read your emails, we don't care about what your email contains (unless it's illegal) and we don't want your data.

Including Swivro-trust

Swivro trust is our promise to you that we do not unethically track you, we do not spy on you, we do not want to know who you are or what you are doing, and we respect your privacy. No data mining, no anti-privacy decisions, no BS. You can trust us.

Hosted in Europe for privacy

The European Union (EU) is known to have great privacy laws. This is why we are choosing to host our email platform in countries part of the EU (specifically, Luxembourg), so your email data is protected by the EU's strong privacy laws.
