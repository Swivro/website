Lack of Free Speech

Various companies & organizations these days do not support free speech and are big on censorship, such as Amazon.

Censorship is big, and many tech giants do not support free-speech. That's bad.

People on the internet are getting censored simply for expressing their thoughts and opinion, just because the censoring-company disagrees or has a different view than the user. This is wrong; everyone on the internet should be able to express their opinion & thoughts.

  • What is the issue?
  • Why should you care?
  • What is the solution?

Various companies such as Google, Microsoft, Amazon, GoDaddy, and more, do not support free speech, and are anti-free-speech. Companies like the ones listed above will cease providing services to a user simply because they don't agree with what the user believes. That is censorship. That's bad. 

People should have the right the express themselves and their thoughts online on the internet without fear of censorship. Imagine if you simply tried sharing your opinion on, say, your website, and next thing you know, your hosting provider suspends your account and your domain is seized due to them disagreeing with your beliefs. You wouldn't like that. 

Thankfully, not all companies out there on the internet are anti-free-speech. There are a handful of companies that are for free speech, including hosting providers, domain registrars, and more. We need to stop companies from censoring people, and we need to promote free-speech, and only use free-speech providers, such as Njalla (for domains and hosting), and more.
