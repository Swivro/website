Restore and retain your online privacy today.

Swivro is an organization focused on restoring your online privacy, helping you speak freely without censorship, as well as helping you anonymize yourself.

If you don't have anything to hide from anyone, why should you even care about your online privacy?

Let's not confuse privacy with secrecy; keeping your privacy is different than keeping a secret. Everyone knows what happens on the toilet, but you still choose to close the bathroom door. That's not because you want to be secretive, you simply want privacy. Everyone has something to hide. Still not convinced?

Explore Privacy Resources

Why should I care about security for my online accounts? If they get hacked it's just whatever.

You should care about your online security for many reasons, most importantly - if your online account(s) gets hacked, it's not just "whatever", as your personal info is now exposed to the public and can be used for fraudulent purposes. Your passwords should be so complicated that you can't remember them.
