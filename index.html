Stay Anonymous Cybersecurity

If you don't have anything to hide from anyone, why should you even care about your online privacy?

Let's not confuse privacy with secrecy; keeping your privacy is different than keeping a secret. Everyone knows what happens on the toilet, but you still choose to close the door. That's not because you want to be secretive of what's going on, you simply want privacy. Everyone has something to hide. Still not convinced?

Why should I care about security for my online accounts? If they get hacked it's just whatever.

You should care about your online account security for many reasons, most importantly; if you're online account gets hacked, it's not just "whatever", your personal and sensitive information is now exposed to the public and can be used for fraudulent purposes. Using just a password isn't enough. Setup 2FA.
