How many people run Swivro?

Swivro is not a business, or a company. Swivro is a 'nonprofit' technology group, or we can be considered an organization. "Nonprofit technology group" means that our primary focus is not to make a profit and we are made up of a few people, but Swivro's official owner is known online as Kasper.

Is Swivro Free?

Swivro itself is not a product, but it owns brands that offer products, like Speaklink. Most of our products are free whilst some are considered a premium service, requiring a payment in order to use the service.

What is Speaklink "v2"?
×
Initially, Speaklink used the IRC Protocol for texting/chatting. Although IRC is great for lots of things, in v2 of Speaklink, we've switched over to the Matrix Protocol. It allows for more features, is more secure, and is fully end-to-end encrypted. Video calling, voice calling, and screen-sharing is now supported, along with a bunch of other features, powered by Element.
