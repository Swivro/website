You should care about your digital privacy. Everyone has something to hide.

Lets not confuse privacy with secrecy; keeping your privacy is different than keeping a secret. Everyone knows what happens on the toilet, but you still choose to close the bathroom door. That's not because you want to be secretive, you want privacy. Everyone has something to hide. Still not convinced?

Imagine if you could make payments internationally, without a central authority.

Say hello to cryptocurrency. There are thousands of different cryptocurrencies, but only certain ones good for DeFi (decentralized finance). Cryptocurrency allows you to make international payments without providing any PII (Personally Identifiable Information), or without having a central authority.
