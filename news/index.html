On August 5th, 2021, Apple Inc. announced new technological measures meant to apply across virtually all of its devices under the umbrella of “Expanded Protections for Children”. While child exploitation is a serious problem, and while efforts to combat it are almost unquestionably well-intentioned, Apple’s proposal introduces a backdoor that threatens to undermine fundamental privacy protections for all users of Apple products. Apple’s proposed technology works by continuously monitoring photos saved or shared on the user’s iPhone, iPad, or Mac. One system detects if a certain number of objectionable photos is detected in iCloud storage and alerts the authorities. Another notifies a child’s parents if iMessage is used to send or receive photos that a machine learning algorithm considers to contain nudity. Because both checks are performed on the user’s device, they have the potential to bypass any end-to-end encryption that would otherwise safeguard the user’s privacy. (credit appleprivacyletter.com) Additionally, Apple essentially, in a way, has access to view any of your photos without you knowing.

You may notice that on some of our brands, products, or services (on our Brands & Services page), have (LTS) marked next to them. LTS simply stands for Long Term Support. Brands/Services on our Brands & Services page which are marked as LTS mean they do not plan to be abandoned (without notice), are very stable & are production ready, and are good enough quality to have Long Term Support (LTS) by Swivro. Our Brands & Services which are not marked as (LTS) do not have Long Term Support and could be shut down at any time. Our Brands & Services which are not marked as LTS, don’t have LTS because it does not meet one or more of the following requirements:

  • Deemed by Swivro to be decent quality
  • Works very well, is stable, and is production-ready
  • Is not in a testing/beta/experimental phase

TL;DR our services that are NOT marked as LTS (Long Term Support) are either still in a testing phase, have plans to be shut down, aren’t good enough quality, or lacks significant features compared to competitors. Our services that ARE marked as LTS are production-ready, work very well, and are stable, planned to have long term support & updates.
