Introducing Nova Search

We’re introducing Nova Search, an upcoming service managed by Swivro. Nova Search is a fast privacy-respecting online search engine. Nova Search is a completely anonymous search engine, it doesn’t track you and it doesn’t sell your data. It is scheduled to be released at the end of this monthy, being July, or early August. The code for Nova Search is ready but we are seeking a hosting provider for Nova Search, and right now our #1 option is Microsoft’s Azure Platform.

We are (likely) choosing Azure because of it’s proven high reliability, security, and high uptime. We’ve been creating experimental search engines throughout 2020 & 2021 but now it’s time to create a public one, available to anyone, online. Say goodbye to Bing & Google.
