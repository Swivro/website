We're Hosting DraugerOS's New York Download Server!

DraugerOS is a linux gaming OS, in fact, in our opinion, it is the best linux gaming OS. Learn more about DraugerOS at their website, accessible via DraugerOS.org. Moving on.. We’re now happily hosting a mirror of the DraugerOS Download Repository, accessible at draugeros-ny1.swivro.net/download.draugeros.org in your web browser. This will be (at least one of) DraugerOS’s New York Download Servers. This will, hopefully, drastically improve download speeds for DraugerOS users based in or near NYC.
