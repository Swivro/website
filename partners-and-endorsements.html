FAQ

Are all of these organizations partners of Swivro?

No, not all of the organizations listed on this page are partners of Swivro. Some or most may be endorsements, aka organizations that we, Swivro, endorse.

Why do you partner with these organizations?

As said before, not all of the organizations listed on this page are partners of ours. We may just endorse them. We partner with organizations only if we both benefit from the partnership.

Can I become a partner?

Unless we reach out to you, no :)
