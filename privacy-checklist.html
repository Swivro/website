Quick Privacy Checklist

1

  Enable automatic computer updates

Always ensure that your system & your software is up to date.
2

 Use Private Search Engines

Instead of using Google, use a private search engine like Startpage.
3

  Use Password Managers

To maximize account security, use a password manager like Bitwarden.
4

  Use 2-step Authentication

Never have a single layer of authentication. Setup 2-step authentication today.
5

  Use Secure Email Providers

Providers like Gmail are not private. Use a private email provider like ProtonMail.
6

  Use Encrypted Messengers

You should use an encrypted messenger like Session to keep your conversations private.
7

  Use a VPN to stay private

A privacy-respecting VPN provider will keep your IP hidden & your browsing habits private.
8

  Backup your data securely

It's always a good idea to backup your computer data to a secure cloud storage provider.
9

  Be smart on the internet

Before clicking a link, sharing info, or signing up for a site, verify that the site/source is safe.
