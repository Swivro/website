Privacy Checklist

This is a quick way to secure yourself on the internet, keep your online privacy, and ensure that you are very secure.

Update Your Software

Always ensure that your system & software is up to date. It's best to enable automatic updates.

     Change Search Engine

    Ensure that you're not using Google, Bing, or Yahoo, and are using a privacy-respecting alternative.

       Secure your passwords

      It's not a good idea to remember a single password for all your accounts. Use a password manager today.

        Use 2-step authentication

        Never just rely on your password to login to your accounts. Setup 2-step authentication today.

           Use encrypted email providers

          Providers like Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, AOL, and more, are all not secure. Use encrypted email today.

             Use an encrypted messenger

            You should avoid holding prolonged conversations via email & using SMS. Discover secure messengers.

              Use a VPN to remain private

              A privacy-respecting VPN provider will keep your IP address hidden and your browsing habits private.

                 Backup your data all the time

                It is always a good idea to backup your data to a secure cloud storage provider in the event of your device failing.

                   Be smart on the internet

                  Before clicking a link, sharing info, or signing up for a website, verify that the site/source is secure & trustworthy.
