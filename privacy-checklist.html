Privacy Checklist

This is a quick way to secure yourself on the internet, retain your online privacy, and ensure that you are very secure.

Update Computer Software

Always ensure that your system & software is up to date. It's best to enable automatic updates.

     Use Private Search Engines

    Ensure you're not using Google, Bing, or Yahoo, and are using a privacy-respecting alternative.

       Use Password Managers

      It's not a good idea to use one password for all your accounts. Use a password manager.

         Use 2-step Authentication

        Never solely rely on your password to login to your accounts. Setup 2-step authentication today.

           Use Secure Email Providers

          Providers like Gmail, AOL, Yahoo, HotMail, and more, are not secure. Use encrypted email today.

             Use Encrypted Messengers

            You should avoid avoid holding prolonged conversations via email. Use secure messengers.

               Use a VPN to stay private

              A privacy-respecting VPN provider will keep your IP address hidden and your browsing habits private.

                 Backup your data securely

                It's always a good idea to very often backup your data to a secure cloud storage provider.

                   Be smart on the internet

                  Before clicking a link, sharing info, or signing up for a site, verify that the site/source is secure & trustworthy.
