Safe Web Hosting

Most hosting providers are not privacy-respecting, and are willing to take down your website due to them being anti-free-speech.

Looking for a secure hosting provider?

Chances are, you may be currently using a hosting provider that is anti-free-speech, does not respect your privacy, and is willing to give in to government requests instantly without thinking. We're here to recommend you some free-speech secure hosting providers that respect your privacy and won't brainlessly give in to government requests.

Option 1: Webdock

To our knowledge, Webdock allows free-speech on websites hosted on their servers, and respects its users' privacy. Webdock provides excellent server hosting at a good price, while simplifying the whole server experience. As they say, ”It's Like Linode™️ and cPanel™️ Had A Beautiful Child" which from our experience is completely true. Webdock has various hosting awards from Hostadvice, is a Stripe Climate Partner, and offers servers for as low as a single euro per month. Additionally, Webdock is fully GDPR compliant and never retains any user information once the user deletes it. They also do not pull any log files from any of their customers' servers. Webdock is also where we host our website!

Option 2: Njalla

Njalla is most famous for their anonymous domain registration offering. As Njalla says, "When you buy a domain in our system, we're actually purchasing it for ourselves. We will be the actual owners of the domain, however, you will still have the full control over the domain name. You can either use our information, our nameservers or you can go with your own. If you ever want to move the domain from our system, we'll of course give you the domain without any additional costs". Their goal is to minimize your visibility (personal details) to the public. As for VPS/Server Hosting, they provide secure servers at a low cost, but we recommend checking out Webdock too.

Option 3: 1984 Hosting

1984 Hosting is mostly popular for their friendliness towards privacy with their preconfigured OpenVPN server offering. When you get a server from 1984 hosting with preconfigured OpenVPN, any VPN logs constantly & immediately go to /dev/null. The VPN Server also uses itself for DNS (a truly private VPN). As 1984 Hosting says, the only true non-logging fully private VPN is the one you have full control over. While they do offer VPS's, they also offer Shared Web Hosting and domain names, but we still recommend njalla for domain names, as they seem to be more focused on privacy-first domain registration.

Option 4: Bahnhof

Based in Sweden, Bahnhof provides low-cost virtual private servers. Bahnhof is privacy-focused, free-speech, and accepts various cryptocurrencies. Like njalla, they don't give into government requests easily, and they are not based in the USA. Server providers based solely in the USA are likely never good for privacy or free-speech, and will give in to government requests in an instant, as we saw with HMA VPN (see more info about HMA VPN's willingness to deface their customers on our malicious company directory page).
