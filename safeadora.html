Letting you know what to worry about

If a destination is moderately safe, we'll let you know why, and what you should be cautious of when traveling there. In the screenshot displayed, you can see that, at the time of writing, you should beware of crime outside of tourist areas in Mexico.

Safety Ratings for destinations

We rank countries/destinations based on crime/terrorism in the area, levels of disease, and a few other factors. The three ratings we provide are "Safe" which is green, "Moderately Safe" which is orange, and "Not Safe" which is red.
