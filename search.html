 Swivro Search

Swivro Search improves your privacy while searching the internet by aggregating search results from many independent search engines such as Google, Bing, Qwant, DuckDuckGo, and more, with complete privacy protection. You get the best results, with no tracking.

Search Anonymously Now

Aggregates results from dozens of search engines

100% Open Source, based on the SearXNG software.

We don't and will never track you. No BS involved.

An encrypted HTTPS/TLS connection is forced.

Including Swivro-trust

Swivro trust is our promise to you that we do not unethically track you, we do not spy on you, we do not want to know who you are or what you are doing, and we respect your privacy. No data mining, no anti-privacy decisions, no BS. You can trust us.

Powered by Open Source

Swivro Search is open source, with the code being available on GitHub. Swivro Search is a fork of SearXNG, which is a fork of the original Searx. Swivro Search is essentially Searx but with UI modifications, additional search engines, and guaranteed privacy.

What is a SearX Node?
×
SearX is an open source search engine written in Python. Anyone can host their own public instance of SearX, which anyone can use (we call those SearX Nodes). Our database consists of many SearX Nodes. When you submit a search query here, your query is sent to one of the SearX instances instances in our database, picked randomly. This improves your privacy and anonymity.
