Secret Share

Secret Share allows you to secretly share self-destructing important messages in a quick, simple, secure, and private way. We don't log any messages and can not know what the contents of your message are. Secret Share is based on open source software.

Secret Share is powered by open source software.

100% Zero-knowledge. We can't see your messages.

Secrets automagically self-destruct after being viewed

Secret Share features multiple layers of encryption.

Introducing Swivro-trust; your privacy is guaranteed.

Swivro trust is our promise to you that we do not unethically track you, we do not spy on you, we do not want to know who you are or what you are doing, and we respect your privacy. No data mining, no anti-privacy decisions, no BS. You can trust us.

Integrated AES-256bit encryption

Secret Share utilizes strong AES 256-bit encryption, as well as transport encryption. Each time you create a new secret, a random 256-bit AES key is created, as well as a random prune date (365-730 days) if the secret is not viewed in time.
