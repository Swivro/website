Safe Crypto Wallets

Send payments to individuals/companies anonymously & securely without exposing your personal information.

Send Payments to people anonymously & securely via cryptocurrency

Enough of sending payments via fiat currency. Send payments to people anonymously by using cryptocurrency, like Monero, Litecoin, etc. To get started, you'll need to get your own Cryptocurrency Wallet. On this page, we'll recommend some secure wallets.

1. Monero Wallet

Monero is the most private & anonymous cryptocurrency, use this if you need anonymity and privacy. Monero Wallet supports Windows, Linux, and Mac OS X. If you're looking for a Monero wallet for mobile devices (Android/iOS), you can have a look at Cake Wallet. To actually get Monero cryptocurrency, we suggest using a crypto exchange company to buy BTC with fiat, then transfer that BTC to your own wallet (IE: Electrum), then use Swapzone.io to exchange that BTC for XMR (Monero), and receive the Monero using your Monero Wallet.

2. Electrum Wallet

Electrum is very simple but lacks many features when compared to other wallets. It does not show real-time graphs, or any graphs at all of the value of different coins/cryptocurrencies, but does the basics; allows you to send and receive cryptocurrency. Electrum is available on Linux, Windows, OSX, and Android. Electrum is a Bitcoin Wallet, and only officially supports Bitcoin. If you are seeking a extremely simple Bitcoin crypto wallet, then Electrum is for you. Check out Electrum at electrum.org. Bitcoin is not as anonymous as Monero, as Bitcoin has many flaws when it comes to anonymity and privacy. If you need maximum privacy & anonymity, please consider using Monero and not Bitcoin.

3. Exodus Wallet

Exodus Crypto Wallet is not a wallet for a single cryptocurrency, it is one central crypto wallet that allows you to store all of your cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Cardano, Monero, and countless others. Exodus also offers a cryptocurrency change service in the Exodus App (where you can exchange X coin for Y coin, but the fees are usually relatively high). Exodus is free of course, and is partially open source software. Exodus is available on PC, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS. Lastly, Exodus features a very elegant, simple, clean, and easy-to-navigate interface. Check out Exodus at exodus.com.
