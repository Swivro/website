Anonymous Payments

Send payments to individuals or companies anonymously, securely, and privately without exposing your personal information.

Send Payments to people anonymously & securely

Enough of sending payments via PayPal and via your credit card directly. Send payments to people anonymously by using cryptocurrency, like Bitcoin, Litecoin, etc. To get started, you'll need to get your own Cryptocurrency Wallet. On this page, we'll recommend some secure wallets. How you get a balance (exchange fiat to crypto) is up to you.

Exodus Wallet

We recommend the Exodus Crypto Wallet to basically everybody. Exodus is very secure and is an app that you download, not an unsecure website. Exodus is not a wallet for one cryptocurrency, it is one central crypto wallet that allows you to store all of your cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Cardano, Dogecoin, Monero, and countless others. Exodus is free of course, and is partially open source software. Exodus is available on PC, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS. Lastly, Exodus features a very elegant, simple, clean, and easy-to-navigate interface. Check out Exodus at exodus.com.

Electrum Wallet

Electrum is very simple but lacks many features when compared to Exodus. It does not show real-time graphs, or any graphs at all of the value of different coins/cryptocurrencies, but does the basics; allows you to send and receive cryptocurrency. Electrum is available on Linux, Windows, OSX, and Android. Electrum is a Bitcoin Wallet, and only officially supports Bitcoin. We still recommend trying Exodus, but if you are seeking a extremely simple Bitcoin-only crypto wallet, then Electrum is for you. Check out Electrum at electrum.org.

Monero Wallet

The name speaks for itself, Monero Wallet is a wallet for the Monero cryptocurrency. Monero Wallet, like Electrum, also lacks many features when compared to Exodus. Monero supports Windows, Linux, and Mac OS X, with no support for Android or iOS. If you're looking for a Monero-only wallet for mobile devices, you can have a look at Cake Wallet (cakewallet.com), but we still recommend Exodus instead. Check out Monero Wallet at getmonero.org.
