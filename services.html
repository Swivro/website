FAQ

Are the services listed on this page all of your services?

No, Swivro does actively maintain other services (other than the ones listed on this page), but they are not our main focus, and we don't publicly associate ourselves with some.

Are all these products free?

No. Most of our products are free, but some may be considered premium services & require a payment in order to begin using them.

How can I contribute?

If you have an idea or want to contribute to Swivro, you can either submit your idea to us via support@swivro.com or you can send us a donation :)

How do you handle security threats?

We have no security policy, meaning we are not obligated to inform you about a discovered security issue within a certain amount of time, however, we do our best to inform you under 72 hours.

How are Swivro's Products different?
×
Swivro is heavily focused on internet privacy and security, and that's why none of our products & services contain the following:

• Mass Surveillance
• Unethical Tracking
• Personalized Ads
• American Servers (hosted in the EU for maximum privacy)
• Browser Fingerprinting
• Web Beacons
• Web Analytics
• Much more

Because we at Swivro are so focused on internet privacy and security, we have made Swivro accessible via Tor for the highest possible anonymity, security, and privacy. You can find the Tor (.onion) link to Swivro at the footer (bottom) of our website.
We're trying to make several dozen privacy-focused products & services, but unfortunately we have limited time & resources, so we can't work on this 24/7. To this date, 17th of August (2021), we are offering multiple privacy-focused products, including a communications platform (similar to Microsoft Teams or Discord), a Password Generator & Manager, a Search Engine, a Website Search Engine Optimization Tool, a website which shows you privacy-focused alternatives to popular software (like Google Drive, Google Chrome, and more), a Pastebin service, and a File Storage Platform (similar to Google Drive). All of those products are available to anyone free of charge, and free of unethical tracking of course. We plan on offering even more Products & Services, but as mentioned, we don't have unlimited free time.
Most of our products & services may use open source software. We do our best to somewhat simplify this open source software to make it easier to use, and for it to be all in one place, on our Products Page.

If you have any suggestions please shoot us an email at support@swivro.com
Do note that we will not reply for security reasons, but we'll review your message within approximately 5 days.
