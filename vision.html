A letter from the creator of Swivro

Colonizing the universe and living in space is something humanity needs to do, and we need to do it as soon as possible, because we don't know when the window of opportunity to begin colonizing the universe will end. We're seeing the Russia and Ukraine conflict happen right now (as of Feb. 27th, 2022), and many people are thinking this could start World War 3. If that happens, humanity is dead. Everybody will nuke each other like idiots. Just another civilization in the cosmos that had so much potential, but found a way to kill themselves. We can't let that happen. We just can't.

Imagine a world where the human race is decentralized, where humanity has no single point of failure, where humanity is thriving in space, where we are living among the stars, exploring the deep universe. There's nothing more inspiring than that. SpaceX is one of few private companies that is actually trying to advance human spaceflight & the human race in general. As Elon Musk (CEO and Chief Engineer of SpaceX) said, SpaceX has a goal of colonizing Mars, while many other aerospace companies have a goal of throwing more satellites up into orbit. He's absolutely correct here - the majority of aerospace companies just have a goal of launching satellites to orbit in different ways. There's no company more important than SpaceX right now, and there's no person more important than Elon Musk right now. By supporting SpaceX, you are supporting a better future for humanity.

Now lets imagine a world where we are forever confined to Earth - our home planet. Even if climate change, overpopulation, high air pollution, a crazy powerful meteor impact, or a nuclear war, all were to never occur, Earth would still be consumed by the constantly-expanding Sun (our star) within the next few hundred million years. The Andromeda Galaxy is also expected to collide with the Milky Way in a few billion years time, which could also wipe out most of humanity if we are still living only in the Milky Way. A window of opportunity has opened, allowing us to begin colonizing the universe, starting with Mars. As mentioned, we don't know when that window will close, which is why we need to act as fast as physically and humanly possible.

Consciousness, based on our current knowledge, is a very rare thing, and we must do everything we possibly can to preserve it. Colonizing Mars is just the first step. Humanity will keep pushing forward and will hopefully be outside of the Milky Way Galaxy in under 3 billion years time. Swivro was originally a programming collective for web apps & privacy-respecting software, but I realized the importance of humanity becoming a multiplanetary & intergalactic species, which is why Swivro's focus has now shifted. 

- Creator of Swivro, known online as Kasper.
