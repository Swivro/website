VPN Speed may vary

Your VPN speed may vary depending on multiple conditions. Your VPN will most certainly have a speed (worst case scenario) of 5 MBPS, but is capable of up to more than 800 MBPS on servers that have a network speed of 1 GBPS. Please note that this is the bandwidth which is "allocated" to each VPN account/tunnel: the bandwidth you can obtain depends also on your provider conditions, peering and several further conditions. To test your home network speed (without VPN), we recommend visiting speedtest.net

VPN Information

Swivro VPN essentially consists of KVM Slices from FranTech with the WireGuard software installed. The difference when you get it through us is, it's all managed for you. System updates, software upgrades, VPN Configuration, and more, is all managed for you, meaning you don't have to worry about a single thing when it comes to managing your VPN. Simply, all you need to do is place an order (on this page) and within 48 hours we will send you the file which you will use to connect to your VPN. It's that simple! You even get a dedicated/unique IP Address and the same dedicated resources as you would if you ordered this directly through FranTech. If you just need a VPN and aren't too fond of Linux or don't know how to use it very well, this product is also for you!
